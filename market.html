Lagos Markets in the Stars

  • Category: Data visualisation / Interactive storytelling
  • Technical Skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, DOM manipulation, CSS animation

Motivation: Markets are central to Nigerian life, shaping communities, local economies, and daily movement. Lagos has dozens, each with its own identity, history, and role. Instead of a standard map, I wanted a more atmospheric way to represent them. That idea became Lagos Markets in the Stars.

Overview: All 67 Lagos markets are shown as an interactive night sky. Each star represents a market, with size and brightness reflecting its importance. Major hubs appear brighter and larger, while smaller community markets appear as subtle points of light, forming a living constellation of the city.

How It Works: Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript without frameworks. Stars are generated across the screen, each storing market name, location, and description. Hover reveals the name, click opens a detail panel, and stars can be dragged or reshuffled. Background stars animate independently to keep the sky dynamic.

Impact: The project turns civic data into something visual and engaging. It helps people explore Lagos markets intuitively and understand their roles, locations, and significance in a more immersive way.
